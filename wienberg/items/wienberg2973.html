Title:
Santa Monica breakwater, Santa Monica Pier, Cal.
Date Created:
1936
Description:
Small boats are anchored in the Yacht Harbor north of the Santa Monica Municipal Pier. The breakwater was built in 1934 to create the yacht harbor, although recreational boaters found the waters choppy and use of the harbor slowly dwindled.
