Title:

Date Created:
1962
Description:
View of Point Mugu, just north/west of Malibu in Ventura County. In 1940, workers used 107 tons of explosives to blast a path through the rock at Point Mugu to make way for the highway that would become the Pacific Coast Highway [source: Nathan Masters, KCET]. Printed on reverse: Coast Highway 101, leaving 'The Malibu,' approaches Split Rock near the famous Naval Station at Point Mugu, California. Color Photography by Max Maran. Written on reverse: Dear Edith- We drove along here on our way to Oxnard- Made L.A. in 4 hrs 50 minutes from New York- Ruth & Harry
