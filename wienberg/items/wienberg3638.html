Title:
Hollywood hosts soldiers
Description:
Written on back: "Castle Rock, Pacific Palisades, CAL" Printed on back: While appearing in Universal's "Broadway," co-starring George Raft and Pat O'Brien, young actresses headed by Janet Bliar conceived the idea of playing host to six soldiers from Camp Callan, Calif., for a holiday in Hollywood. Among features of the full day of recreation was a visit to the beach at Santa Monica. Frolicking are (L to R) Janet Bliars and Marjorie Lord on the shoulders of their husky guests.
