Title:
Serra Point and Seaward View, Serra Retreat, Malibu, Calif. 183
Date Created:
1949
Description:
This photo postcard shows the original Serra Retreat in the foothills near Malibu Creek in central Malibu. The view is of Serra Point with its cross and Malibu Creek and Malibu Colony in the background. Named for JunÃ­pero Serra, the Apostle of California, Serra Retreat was purchased from the Rindge estate in 1942 by the Franciscan Order of California. Although the original buildings were destroyed in a 1970 wildfire, Serra Retreat rebuilt on the same location and remains a religious retreat and conference center today.
