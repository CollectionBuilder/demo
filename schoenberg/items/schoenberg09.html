Title:
Book of Hours (England, mid-14th century), Folio 2 of 2
Date:
Mid-14th century
Description:
Single parchment folio ruled in red ink, written in a single column of twelve lines in black ink. Rubrication but no initials or pen flourishing as on 104.1, except for a small amount of faded purple flourishing (originally blue) around a paraph mark (Â¶) on the verso. Each side contains a single 2-line illuminated initial (recto: E; verso: D) on a field of pink and blue with white detailing.
Physical Description:
Single parchment folio
Language:
Latin
Genre:
Manuscripts, Latin--14th century
Note:
Script: Mechanical, angular Gothic textura (akin to Brown's "English Gothic Book Script," WHS pl. 28). Highly vertical; severe biting; numerous hairline serifs (esp. on tironian 'et' abbreviation; terminal 't'; split tail of 'p'; ascender of 'l').
