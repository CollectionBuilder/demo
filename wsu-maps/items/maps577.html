Title:
Post route map of the state of Washington : showing post offices with the intermediate distances on mail routes in operation on the 1st of September 1905 under the direction of A. von Haake, (1905)
Date Created:
1905
Description:
1 map : col. ; 77 x 106 cm. Also shows frequency of delivery. Relief shown by spot heights. Seal of the Post Office Department, United States of America at head of title. Insets: Seattle and vicinity, Tacoma and vicinity. Includes list of counties showing relative position within state. Mounted on cloth backing. The colored lines on the map show frequency of mail delivery as noted in the key at the bottom right of the map. Mail service could be as rare as just once a week in some areas.

[Washington?] : Published by order of Postmaster General Geo. B. Cortelyou
