Title:
White's Atlas of Pierce County Washington, Tp. 18 N., R. 3 E., W. M., Wash. (1928)
Creator:
White, D. H. (David H.)
Date Created:
1928
Description:
1 map; 66 x 37 cm. Scale 1" =1/4 mile. Shows additions, lot numbers, street names, railroads, section numbers, and names of some landowners. This map is part of D. H. White's Atlas of Pierce County, Washington, which consists mainly of facing pages of map sheets which depict the Eastern or Western portions of specific Township Ranges of Pierce County.

Tacoma, Wa. : D. H. White, C.E.
