Title:
North America--West Coast, Strait of Juan de Fuca and the channels between the continent & Vancouver Id., showing the boundary line between British & American possessions : from the Admiralty Surveys by Captains H. Kellett, R.N., 1847 & G.H. Richards, R.N., 1858-62 (1862). Strait of Juan de Fuca and the channels between the continent & Vancouver Id. (1862)
Creator:
Great Britain. Hydrographic Office. Powell, Edward J.
Date Created:
1874
Description:
1 map : hand col. ; 71 x 112 cm. "Drawn by Edward J. Powell of the Hydrographic Office." Depths shown by soundings. Relief shown by hachures and spot heights. "Soundings in fathoms." Shows detailed topography and bathymetry of the Straits of Juan de Fuca and waters between Straits of Georgia and Admiralty Inlet. Identifies islands, coastal features, reefs. banks, kelp beds, point of anchorage and mountain peaks. Also gives information on lighthouses, bottom types and tides. Boundary line is shaded red on the side of British possessions, blue on the side of the American possessions. Indexed in: CIS US serial set index, pt. 14, 1594 (43-1) H.exdoc. 1, map 1. Scale ca. 1:165,000 (W 124&deg;5000--W 122&deg;2500/N 49&deg;0500--N 48&deg;0500)

Washington : Government Printing Office.
